Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Controller

RetreatX Costa Rica, operated by iPresidency, is the controller of your personal data. For questions please contact privacy@retreatx.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data you voluntarily provide, such as your e-mail address when you request a Magic Link. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics without your explicit consent.

3. Purpose & Legal Basis

Your e-mail address is used solely to send you a passwordless login link (legitimate interest & performance of a contract, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR). Documents you upload to the Secure Vault are stored encrypted and used only to provide the service to you.

4. Storage & Security

All personal data is stored on servers located within the European Union. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We apply industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.2+, AES-256 encryption, and regular security audits.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your data, and to object to its processing. To exercise any right please write to privacy@retreatx.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts (loaded from fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com), which may collect your IP address. For payment processing we use Stripe and accept cryptocurrency via Web3 wallets — both are subject to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.
